ROOTS

I just thought that I didn't want to spend a normal life.
I wanted to make something bloom in my life.
So I wanted to start a business.

To that end, I started working on various businesses and products.
But it didn't work.
I didn't know why it didn't work, and I just lost my trust.

I met a certain person at such time.
He pointed out my bad personality.
Selfishness, passive, uncaring, subjective, serial thinking, negative thinking
So he told me that no matter what I did, it didn't work.
I've thought that's right.
I felt fascinated by his love and decided to follow.

He also taught the principles, laws, mechanisms, and distortions of this world.
He was the one who accepted both the front and back worlds.
It embodies altruism, pioneering, compassion, objective, parallel thinking and positive thinking.

Guided by that, I have begun to follow the management path.
Next is my turn to guide my juniors.
To create a future where everyone's dreams can be realized.
